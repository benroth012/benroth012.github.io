Experiences

Prosthetic Hand Project

I was lucky enough to be able to work in a group of 4 with some of my friends to work on something for our community. We were able to help a four year old in our community named Hansika who was suffering from a genetic condition called symbrachydactyly. She was born with one fully developed hand and another that did not form properly. My group worked together over the full school year to build a prosthetic hand for her, there was a lot of research needed, trial and error, and loads of prototypes.

We were on the news!
