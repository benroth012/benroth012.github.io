About me

Hi, my name is Ben Roth, and I am a Computer Science and Engineering student with a minor in Entrepreneurship and Innovation at The Ohio State University. I grew up in both Cincinnati, Ohio and Greenville, South Carolina. I have always had a strong interest in math, technology, and helping people solve problems. I enjoy playing video games, watching sports, and working out. I strongly value the time that I have with friends and family, because they are extremely important to me in everything that I do. I really enjoy coding and working with technology, but it isn’t the only thing that I am passionate about. I hope to be able to use my knowledge in technology to help make progress in the fight against climate change. Even though I am not majoring in anything environment related, I have a strong desire to address this issue. I would also love to help combat social injustices in any way that I can. Going forward in my career, I hope to work on projects that I am passionate about, ventures that inspire and motivate me every single day. I would love to start my own company or create something to make an impact on the world.
